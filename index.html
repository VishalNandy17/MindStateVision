Frequently Asked Questions

Is MSV a replacement for professional therapy?

No, MSV is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. We provide supportive tools and resources, but always recommend consulting with a qualified healthcare provider for personalized advice.

How is my data protected?

We take your privacy seriously. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your personal information without your explicit consent.

Can I use MSV for free?

Yes, we offer a free basic version with access to essential features. Premium features are available through a paid subscription.
